Frequently Asked
questions

What can I transfer from my old phone?
Texts, contacts, photos and more will be carried over. Check the comprehensive list of what is and isn't transferred during the switching process on the Pixel Phone help page

Are Google apps better on a Pixel?
Your Pixel comes with your favourite Google apps, like Gmail, Maps and Photos, for a seamless experience. And you get Google's latest innovations from popular Google apps first and free.

Is it difficult to transfer data from my old phone to Pixel?
Switching to Pixel doesn't mean starting over. All you have to do is pair or connect your old phone with your new Pixel, then your selected data like contacts, photos, messages and more will copy over. Your new Pixel will guide you through the whole process, which takes about 30 minutes.14,16
